Who Can Benefit from Green Path?

Our platform serves multiple user groups with tailored features and benefits.

❤️

For Customers

  • ✔️ Report waste and schedule convenient pickups
  • ✔️ Track pickup status in real-time
  • ✔️ Donate unused items to those in need
  • ✔️ Join community events and earn social points
  • ✔️ Share experiences and environmental knowledge
🏪

For Dealers

  • ✔️ Accept waste pickup requests
  • ✔️ Guide customers on proper waste segregation
  • ✔️ Track completed pickups and build reputation
  • ✔️ Join local environmental initiatives
  • ✔️ Collaborate with organizations on waste solutions
🏢

For Organizations

  • ✔️ Manage waste reports on a Kanban board interface
  • ✔️ Create and organize community cleanup events
  • ✔️ Assign tasks to volunteers and track progress
  • ✔️ Request specific item donations based on needs
  • ✔️ Access impact analytics and community data
